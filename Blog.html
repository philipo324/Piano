Welcome to Music 2

July, 3, 2015

Today, we are reviewing Fur Elise by Beethoven:

The piece start off very melodic with the iconic two note melody. The pianoist here plays very artisticly: with the two note slurs while at the same time manging to make the iconic melody stand out by accenting the second note of the melody. The piece is played beautifully with no rushing. The player manages to do everything perfectly: dynamics, tempo, and just playing very beautifully and artiscally overall.
